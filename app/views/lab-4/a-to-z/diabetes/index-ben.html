Diabetes

Diabetes is a lifelong condition that causes a person's blood sugar level to become too high. There are different types of diabetes.

Type 2 diabetes

The most common type of diabetes. Includes symptoms, treatment, guidance and support.

Type 1 diabetes

Includes symptoms, support and help for people newly diagnosed.

Pre-diabetes

Learn how to help prevent diabetes, if your blood sugar levels are high.

Gestational diabetes

Diabetes that happens during pregnancy. Includes symptoms, effects and treatments.
