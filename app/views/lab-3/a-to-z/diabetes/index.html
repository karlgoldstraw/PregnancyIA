Diabetes

Diabetes is a lifelong condition that causes a person's blood sugar level to become too high. There are different types of diabetes:

Pre-diabetes

Many more people have blood sugar levels above the normal range, but not high enough to be diagnosed as having diabetes.

Type 1 diabetes

Type 1 diabetes causes the level of glucose (sugar) in your blood to become too high.

Type 2 diabetes

Type 2 diabetes occurs when where the body does not produce enough insulin, or the body's cells do not react to insulin.

Gestational diabetes

Gestational diabetes is high blood sugar (glucose) that develops during pregnancy and usually disappears after giving birth.
